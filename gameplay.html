Gameplay What is the game?

The Mood of Night Vale

On the surface, Night Vale it's a desert town which gives the feeling of a hair over your eye that you just can't seem to brush off. Something just beyond view that's horriably wrong. And once you scratch a little part of the surface, you can only begin to see that Night Vale contains many secrets.
