Influences

The following games share some influence to the game play in certain aspects.

  • Gameplay:
    • The Walking Dead by Telltale Games
    • BEYOND: Two Souls by Quantic Dream
    • Stacking by Double Fine Productions
    • Gone Home by The Fullbright Company
    • The Stanley Parable by Galactic Cafe
  • Art Style:
    • The Walking Dead by Telltale Games

The Mood of Night Vale

Night Vale is a culture amalgamation of X-Files, The Twilight Zone, with a dash of occult.
