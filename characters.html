Characters Who you'll meet in Night Vale.

Principal Characters

You The Player Character

The character of You is predominately featured in the episode "A Story About You" where Cecil narrates a day in You's life, broadcasted for all to hear.

The Player Character

You is not the player. He is an actual character in Night Vale who is only referred to in second person.

You's malleability to the narrative makes him the logical choice as the character the player controls and sees the narrative though; Also known as the Player Character. All other named and known characters have a unique enough personality or mystery in the narrative which would complicate the experience for listeners of the podcast. You is an outsider that live in Night Vale with the explicit purpose of reinventing himself.

Cecil Gershwin Palmer The Voice of Night Vale

The Voice of Night Vale, Cecil is the star character of the Night Vale series. With his unique and welcoming voice, Cecil has a positive disposition to his listeners even when the end of the world is upon the community -- which happens rather often in Night Vale.
